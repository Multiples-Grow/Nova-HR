Mission Control

The core philosophy and operational roles for the $2M Seed Round.

Philosophy: Lines, not Dots

Fundraising is a momentum game. Isolated meetings ("dots") do not raise capital. Consistent, high-frequency updates and progress tracking build the "line" that investors buy into.

Core Principle: "If it is not in the Portal, it did not happen."

The Weekly Sprint

Strict weekly cadence to maintain momentum without burnout.

Operational Rhythm

Sunday Planning & Assignment
Mon-Wed High Volume Outreach
Thursday Cleanup & Review

Sunday: Planning (The Setup)

  • Multiples: Upload new prospect list by 10:00 AM.
  • NovaHR: Review "New Prospects" view in portal.
  • NovaHR: Assign owners (CEO/Co-founder).
  • NovaHR: Enrich with notes on mutual connections (Egypt/KSA ecosystem).

Monday – Wednesday: Outreach (The Grind)

  • Goal: Move assigned prospects to "Outreach" / "Connected".
  • Action: Execute Channel Strategy (LinkedIn -> Email).
  • RULE Do not let assigned leads sit untouched past Wednesday.

Thursday: Review & Cleanup (Housekeeping)

  • Follow-Up Sweep: Check "Action Required" view. Clear pending follow-ups.
  • Update Multiples: Tag @Multiples in portal comments for blockers.
  • RULE No new cold outreach today. Draft for Sunday instead.

Engine & Rules

The operational logic that drives the fundraise.

The Traffic Light System

Green (< 5 days) Investor is active or just contacted. Hidden from "Action Required".
Red (> 5 days) Stagnant deal. Action Required: Send Bump or Value-Add.

The 3-Touch Rule

Touch 1: The Pitch

Initial Teaser + Deck.

Touch 2: The Bump (Day +4)

Short, transactional check-in.

Touch 3: Value Add (Day +10)

Share new win/milestone.

No Reply? -> Nurture

Move to long-term mailing list.
